Bookshelf

As a kid, in my youth, and during my studies I used to read a lot for pleasure — but with a full-time job and a toddler I rarely take the time.

So in early 2020, I made a decision to read more books. This part of my site serves as a reminder to myself and an open bookshelf to everyone else. Enjoy!
